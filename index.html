Starting in the U.S. Caribbean in 2014, we have been developing and fine tuning a suite of tools that can be easily used by fishers, community members, and fisheries professionals to collect reliable data in the field.

Recent advances in computer vision technology provides a new opportunity to process large amounts of high-quality data that can be submitted from members of all communities. We have developed a platform which capitalizes on these technological advancements and creates an efficient and simple data collection process which can be used by virtually anyone.

Today, the FAST Platform is giving fishers, community members, and fisheries professionals around the world a quick and easy tool to collect more and higher quality data for actionable science. In the process, the FAST Platform is developing into a core technology with emerging applications in fisheries biology.

THE FUTURE IS FAST

Our Integrated System

The FAST Platform is an integrated suite of technologies for automatically collecting fisheries landings data. Because sampling environments can be challenging, each component of our system was designed with two goals in mind: increase the speed of sampling to minimize the burden on fishers and markets, and allow for reliable, defensible data to be collected by minimally trained individuals.

In the fisheries where the FAST Platform has been deployed, users are seeing a dramatic increase in the amount of data a single sampler can collect, as well as improved management due to the data's trustworthiness and simplicity.

Hardware

Automated image and weight capture

Smart Sampling Stations capture weight (if needed) and an image of each fish. From images, length, weight, time, and location are automatically generated later.

Stations are custom built to fit any field situation and keep fish flowing. Dockside, on a ramp or beach, in a factory, or at sea, you can get the data FAST:

  • 2 seconds/fish with weight
  • Video even faster if do not need weight

Software

Simple app for error free data entry

An app is loaded on the same device used in the Smart Sampling Station to capture images. The app is designed so that anyone who can push a button can collect data. No training and no writing required.

Data are stored locally in the app, quality controlled on entry, and uploaded to database when sampler has connectivity.

  • Data entry errors virtually eliminated
  • Custom fields and functionalities
  • Staged rollouts to subsets of users

Web Interface

Verify and access data

A web-based user portal provides data access, as well as a backend interface for AI-assigned species ID and length points.

Validated data are available almost immediately for analyses and adaptive management advice. Tune and validate a custom AI/ML algorithm for your fishery.

  • Multiple redundancies to prevent data loss
  • Global standards for encryption and authentication for complete confidence against unauthorized access

To be nimble and applicable...

...to every fishery that is interested in using the FAST Platform, we start with the identification and length determination algorithms from other locations. As partners initiate a new data collection program, each image will be identified to species by a local expert until a representative sample of the top 20–30 species are collected. We then use a process called transfer learning to "tune" our algorithm to the reference library of the species of interest, at which point we provide partners with an open source code specific to their fishery.
